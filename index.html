Expectations and Reality

We are the species having the highest emotional quotient on this planet. Our degree of self- awareness, emotional-intelligence and empathy, individualizes us from our fellow folks.

We seek our own comfort and pleasures without catering towards the needs of others. Not realizing the reality of Newton’s third law ‘For every action there’s an equal and opposite reaction’, in other words ‘As you sow so shall you reap!’

People find serenity in their own profit but cannot find sufficiency until they learn to control their exaggerated expectations and desires. Henceforth, people try to grab more than what they could be genuinely satisfied in. This crazy rat race of garnering admiration and popularity from the social peers drags the person away from the reality of sufficiency in unconditional and faithful bonds.
